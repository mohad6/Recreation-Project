Frequently asked questions:

What exactly is freeCodeCamp?

FreeCodeCamp is a community of people around the world who learn to code together. We have a 100% free curriculum.

How will freeCodeCamp help me learn to code?

You will learn to code by completing coding challenges, building projects, and earning certificates. You will also learn by reading our articles, watching our videos, and joining our forum where you can ask questions.

Is freeCodeCamp really free?

Yes. Every aspect of freeCodeCamp is 100% free. This means, the projects, the videos and the certifications.

Can freeCodeCamp help me get a job as a software developer?

Yes. Many of our alumni have gone on to get jobs as software developers after completing the freeCodeCamp curriculum and getting their first software developer job.

What skills will I learn?

You will learn skills that will help you become a full-stack web developer. You will learn how to code in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, Linux, Git, SQL, and more. You will learn how to build projects and earn certifications that show you have developed real-world coding skills and that you are ready to get a coding job.

How long does it take to learn all this?

freeCodeCamp is self-paced. You can take as long as you need. Some people complete our entire curriculum in a few months, while others take several years to finish.

How do I get started?

If you’re a beginner, you can start at the beginning of the freeCodeCamp curriculum. If you’re more advanced, we recommend starting at the coding project that you are most comfortable with.

How often are there new articles and videos?

We have new articles, videos, and tutorials published every day. You can get all of the latest updates by subscribing to our newsletter and following us on social media.

How do I get help if I’m stuck on a freeCodeCamp curriculum item?

Ask questions in the freeCodeCamp community. We have thousands of freeCodeCamp alumni who are happy to help. You can also read our forum.

Happy coding!
